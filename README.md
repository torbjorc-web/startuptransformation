Startup Transformation
This project analyzes a startup's financial data, expense breakdown, employee productivity, and commute times using Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, and NumPy.

What it does
Examines six months of revenue and expense data.

Visualizes revenue and expenses with line charts.

Analyzes company expense categories with pie charts.

Collapses small expense categories into an Other group.

Sorts employees by productivity and identifies the least productive employees.

Explores the relationship between commute times and log transformation.

Notes
Revenue trends downward while expenses trend upward.

Salaries are the largest expense category.
